## 🚀 AnchorGrid — Remote Support for MSPs & IT Teams

A fast, secure remote-support platform built to go head-to-head with Splashtop and TeamViewer. Solo-built, from the WebRTC transport layer all the way up to the App Store listing.

**Why it's different:**
- ⚡ **Lower latency** — WebRTC P2P with TURN fallback; benchmarks beat Splashtop on LAN (~41–50ms vs ~61ms)
- 🔒 **Trust & safety first** — multi-tenant RBAC, workspace impersonation controls, verified badges, audit logs
- 🧩 **Real iOS screen sharing** — full transport pipeline runs inside a ReplayKit broadcast extension (the correct way, not the `audio`-background-mode hack)
- 🔑 **M365 SSO**, TOTP 2FA, and session health scoring with AI-generated narratives
- 💸 **Honest pricing** — free tier + Pro at $19/mo ($15/mo annual)
